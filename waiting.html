How ORIMI shows up

Not a reminder app.
A doula.

Every message ORIMI sends serves one of five purposes. If it doesn't serve one of these, ORIMI doesn't send it.

01
Educate
Teach her what's really happening
Not "baby is the size of a mango." Baby's lungs are producing surfactant right now. Specific to her week, her body, her baby.
02
Prepare
Before every appointment
48 hours before the glucose test — what the drink tastes like, not to eat sugar beforehand, and the exact questions to ask.
03
Support
Hold space. Validate. Stay.
"That sounds really hard" before "here's what might help." The thing no app does well but every mama needs.
04
Advocate
Give her the words
The BRAIN framework. Teaching her she has the right to ask why — and that asking is not being difficult. It's being an informed parent.
05
Anticipate
Before she has to ask
At 13 emotional flashpoints — anatomy scan, week 37 labor fear, day 3 postpartum crash — ORIMI reaches out first.
