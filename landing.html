For the partner too

ORIMI doesn't just support mom.
She equips her partner.

A partner who knows what to expect, how to advocate, and when to simply hold space isn't just emotionally helpful. They're physiologically helping her labor progress. Oxytocin — the hormone that drives labor — flows when she feels safe, supported, and unobserved.

↑ Oxytocin
Continuous support during labor promotes maternal relaxation, reduces stress hormones, and enhances oxytocin release — directly contributing to cervical dilation and effective contractions
Frontiers in Endocrinology, 2021
↓ Cesarean
Women with continuous support are significantly less likely to need a cesarean, less likely to use pain medication, and more likely to give birth spontaneously with shorter labors
Cochrane Review
16/18
